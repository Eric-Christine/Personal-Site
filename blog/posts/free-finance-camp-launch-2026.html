Free Finance Camp Launch Update

Free Finance Camp is officially live at freefinancecamp.com.

I launched the first production release of Free Finance Camp, a financial literacy web app focused on practical skills with interactive learning. This update includes the custom domain cutover and a cleaner public launch surface for users and search engines.

What shipped in this launch

  • Custom domain: freefinancecamp.com
  • Core SEO setup: canonical tags, social metadata, sitemap, and robots.txt
  • Public-facing lesson and progress flows for smoother onboarding

Why this matters

The launch makes the project easier to find, easier to share, and easier to maintain as content expands. Moving from a temporary hosting URL to a permanent brand domain also sets up cleaner analytics, better trust, and stronger long-term discoverability.

What I am iterating on next

  • Expanding curriculum depth across budgeting, investing, and debt modules
  • Tightening lesson UX and pacing based on user behavior
  • Continuing SEO/content improvements as new lessons go live
